About Starq Industriez

Starq Industriez was founded in March 2026 by a 17-year-old student from India. Built during summer break, the company was born from a simple idea:

"Software should be useful, affordable, and just work — no subscriptions, no bloat."

🚀 Our Mission

To build offline-first, one-time-payment utilities for everyday Windows users. We believe you shouldn't pay every month to keep your PC clean, secure, or productive.

⚡ Our Expertise

We build offline-first Windows utilities that solve real-world problems. Our expertise spans across multiple categories:

🛡️ Cybersecurity – USB threat detection, real-time file monitoring, and system integrity tools.

📹 Media & Downloaders – Video, audio, and playlist downloaders for every platform.

🧹 System Optimization – Junk cleaners, registry fixers, and performance boosters.

🎵 Creative & Productivity – Focus music generators, file organizers, and utility suites.

📂 File Management – Mergers, splitters, compressors, and format converters.

And this is just the beginning. We are constantly expanding our catalog to cover every digital need.

🔥 One rule: Pay once. Own it forever. No subscriptions. No monthly fees. No hidden charges.
